TWILIGHT

Twilight is when the Sun is no longer there to shed light on the obvious, yet lingering long enough to allow us to get a glimpse of reality. Through these images, I wanted to explore what happens in places where people are not there to see. I created mysterious humanoid figure to serve as representations of the things we fail to see.
