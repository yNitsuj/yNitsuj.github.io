NOW IS THE TIME

Now is the Time" is like someone motivating themself for a leap of faith. A person is depicted jumping into a void of the unknown. As that person jumps into the unknown, everything starts to become abstracted as they continue downwards. The construct of time starts to become nonexistent, warping the linear perception of time. It becomes cyclic and linear at the same time. It warps time to the point where the person would end up where they just started. In order to create this, I used After Effects for the animated portions, which are paired with a soundtrack that is discernible when played in forward and reverse.
