DAYDREAMS

I always enjoy looking out the window during journeys because the views I see help stimulate my mind. Through this video, I wanted to represent how my eyes view the world by visualizing the daydreams I have throughout a train ride. By illustrating and animating active lines that play with the environment, I can show the viewer the details that I am focusing on as I create forms out of them. To animate these lines, I drew on each frame individually to create an illusion of movement with the end result.
