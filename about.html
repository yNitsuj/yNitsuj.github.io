About

I am a graphic designer and photographer based in the greater New York region. I enjoy the process of creative brainstorming and producing pieces that will be seen and interpreted by an audience, which gives me a feeling of accomplishment.

I am skilled in using a majority of Adobe Creative Suite programs such as After Effects, Illustrator, Indesign, Lightroom, Photoshop, and Premiere Pro. Using HTML, CSS, and some Javascript, I have designed websites, digital tools, and data visualizations.
