ADAPTING TO A NEW CLIMATE

Being that the lecture series promoted methods of adaptation to our climate, I decided to create a visual narrative with various typographic forms to resemble the action of two environments coming in and interlocking with each other. Using of highly contrasting colors, I am able to create two forms of type that are distinct yet complementary.
